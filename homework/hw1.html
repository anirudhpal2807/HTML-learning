Discover Prayagraj

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a historic and
culturally significant city in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.
Situated at the confluence of three rivers – the Ganges, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati
it holds immense religious, historical, and cultural importance.
Here’s a guide to discovering Prayagraj

Top activities in prayagrag

  • Take a Holy Dip at the Triveni Sangam
  • Explore Allahabad Fort
  • Visit Anand Bhavan and Swaraj Bhavan
  • Attend the Magh Mela or Kumbh Mela
  • Pay a Visit to Hanuman Mandir
  • Take a Stroll at Chandra Shekhar Azad Park
